About Me

This is what I do.

I was born in Mesa, Arizona, I lived in California for a couple of years and them moved back to Arizona when I was five. I graduated from High School and went to a summer semester at Brigham Young University before leaving on my mission. I started my mission in Hamilton, New Zealand and went to Vancouver, Washington on reassignment.

I returned home from my mission in August of 2021 and am now currently attending school at Brigham Young University. I am planning on majoring in Entrepreneurship.

Earlier this year I started a buisness: Lifeguardz Dry Supplies. We got sick and tired of having sandy towels at the beach and knew something had to be done. We sell microfiber towels that are sand-resistant, they pack down super small and are perfect for travel. We are on a mission to fight human trafficking, ten percent of proceeds to go fighting human trafficking around the world.
